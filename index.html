Introduction

Welcome to Flowly — a color-sort puzzle game developed by chAs Tech Group. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use Flowly, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

By downloading or playing Flowly you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

Information We Collect

Advertising Data ADMOB

Flowly uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID, IP address, device identifiers, and general location (country/region) to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance.

Purchase Data IN-APP PURCHASES

If you purchase a Remove Ads plan, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. Flowly does not receive or store your payment card details. We only store the expiry timestamp of your purchase locally on your device.

Game Progress LOCAL ONLY

Your game data — completed levels, hints remaining, daily streak, and settings — is saved only on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

✦ Flowly does not require account registration. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information directly.

How We Use Information

  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob and generate revenue that keeps Flowly free
  • To verify and apply in-app purchases (Remove Ads plans)
  • To save your game progress locally on your device
  • To improve ad relevance via the Advertising ID (only if you have not opted out)

Advertising ID & Opt-Out

Flowly uses the Android Advertising ID (GAID) via AdMob to show relevant ads. You can reset or opt out of personalised advertising at any time:

  • Open your device Settings
  • Go to Google → Ads
  • Tap "Delete advertising ID" or toggle "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"

You will still see ads after opting out, but they will not be personalised.

Data Retention

Game progress and purchase expiry data stored on your device is retained until you uninstall Flowly or clear the app's data through Android Settings.

Data collected by Google AdMob and Google Play is governed by Google's own retention policies. chAs Tech Group does not operate any server-side database for Flowly user data.

Children's Privacy

Flowly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

AdMob is configured to serve general-audience ads. We do not use child-directed ad settings.

Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit personal data to external servers. Network requests made by the app are limited to ad loading (AdMob), purchase verification (Google Play Billing), and connectivity checks (to public endpoints such as connectivitycheck.gstatic.com).

While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we follow industry best practices and rely on Google's infrastructure for all sensitive operations.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to:

  • Access the data held about you by third-party services (Google)
  • Request deletion of your Advertising ID or opt out of personalised ads
  • Clear all locally stored game data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns or requests

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Flowly after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
